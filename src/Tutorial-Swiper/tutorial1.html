Dakee was created to give the real, non-touristic experience in a visited city. Based on your interests, we connect Travellers and Locals to exchange ideas and give tips on what is actually worth to do and see. No tourist cliché!

Who is
a Traveller?

A Traveller looks for a matching Local personto get tips via chat on what to see, visit or do in a specific city. He or she can later give the avaliations for the tips given.

Who is
a Local?

A Local is a person who lives in a city and wants to give some tips to visitors. He or she recieves later avaliations from the Travellers. Each avaliation has a point value that can be exchanges for many rewards and discounts from our Partners.

Be both!

As a Dakee user, you can choose to be both Traveller and Local. Gain discounts by giving advices to visitors and get the unforgettable local experience while travelling!
